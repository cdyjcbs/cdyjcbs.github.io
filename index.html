Cody Jacobs Vocal Studio

About Cody

Cody Jacobs is passionate about helping students of all levels discover their voice and grow as performers. Born and raised in Illinois, he attended the University of Illinois, where he studied vocal performance. Since graduating, he has dedicated himself to mastering his own voice and sharing that knowledge with his students through personalized and engaging instruction.

As the Music Director at Roaming Gnome Theatre and a private voice instructor, he honed his skills in directing, music direction, and teaching. His approach to voice combines mental, physical, and vocal training, emphasizing that a deep understanding of all three is essential for authentic and effortless performance. He is a dedicated student of Estill Voice Training, developed by the late Jo Estill, and continues to explore advanced techniques to stay at the forefront of vocal and bodywork methodologies.

Cody’s mission is to create a supportive environment that nurtures creativity, confidence, and technical excellence. He constantly seeks opportunities to deepen his expertise by working with top professionals in the industry. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced performer, he is committed to helping you achieve your musical and performance goals.

What I Offer

Voice Lessons

Cody offers voice lessons to students of all ages, tailored to individual needs and goals. Lessons are available both in-person and online via Zoom, providing flexibility and accessibility for all.

Audition Preparation

Whether you’re auditioning for a local theatre production, Denver School of the Arts, a college program, or a professional opportunity, Cody provides personalized guidance to help you shine and perform with confidence.

Small Group Workshops

Cody specializes in leading small group workshops, focusing on essential skills such as finding and mastering the contemporary mixed belt, enhancing vocal technique, and building confidence in group settings.

Musical Vocal Direction

With a passion for musical theatre, Cody works closely with casts and vocalists to understand the unique sound of each production, ensuring cohesive and powerful performances through expert vocal direction.

If you’re interested in working with Cody, reach out via the contact form or email at the bottom of this page. You can also log in or sign up for the Student Portal to check his availability.
