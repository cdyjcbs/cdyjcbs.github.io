Cody Jacobs Vocal Studio

About Me

Hello! I'm Cody Jacobs

As a vocal instructor and music director, I am passionate about helping students of all levels discover their voice and grow as performers. With years of experience in voice and acting coaching, I focus on creating a supportive and engaging environment that nurtures creativity and confidence. Let's work together to achieve your musical goals!

What I Offer

Voice Lessons

Cody is happy to provide voice lessons to students of all ages via in-person lessons and is now offering Zoom lessons!

Audition Prep

Whether you are auditioning for a show at a local theatre, the DSA Theatre or Vocal Majors, a College Audition, or a professional audition, Cody can help!

Small Group Workshops

Cody loves to teach small group workshops, particularly for finding and understanding the contemporary mixed belt.

Musical Vocal Direction

Cody enjoys coaching casts and vocalists through shows, understanding the sound quality of each production and translating it effectively.
