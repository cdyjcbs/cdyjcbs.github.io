Cody Jacobs Vocal Studio

About Me

Hello! I'm Cody Jacobs

As a vocal instructor and music director, I am passionate about helping students of all levels discover their voice and grow as performers. With years of experience in voice and acting coaching, I focus on creating a supportive and engaging environment that nurtures creativity and confidence. Let's work together to achieve your musical goals!

What I Offer

Voice Lessons

Cody is happy to provide voice lessons to students of all ages via in-person lessons and is now offering Zoom lessons!

Audition Prep

Whether you are auditioning for a show at a local theatre, the DSA Theatre or Vocal Majors, a College Audition, or a professional audition, Cody can help! From helping you find the right song and monologue to coaching and polishing your final audition, he is here to help!

Small Group Workshops

Cody loves to teach small group workshops, particularly for finding and understanding the contemporary mixed belt. Whether it is your cast of a show or just a small group of friends who are interested in learning, let me know!

Musical Vocal Direction

Cody loves the opportunity to put his skills to use in coaching cast and vocalists through shows. It is an exciting challenge to learn and understand the sound quality associated with each show and figure out how to translate the idea and sound to individuals and ensembles alike.
